Reverence on Withering

With a stance of grace and dignity, we acknowledge the transient nature of the object or experience. Do we choose to embrace the fleeting beauty, allowing ourselves to be enveloped in a bittersweet embrace of remembrance?

We celebrate the beauty found in embracing the transient nature of existence with grace and dignity. Through our curated exhibitions, we invite visitors to contemplate the fleeting nature of objects and experiences, finding solace in the bittersweet embrace of remembrance. Central to our mission is the use of withering objects (mainly flowers, but also personal items, food...), as a poignant symbol of this ethos. Like the withering petals of a flower, we recognize the quiet strength in accepting the inevitability of fading, while cherishing the enduring essence that lingers in our hearts and minds. We aim to evoke contemplation, appreciation, and reverence for the delicate balance between impermanence and enduring beauty.
